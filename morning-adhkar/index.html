أَذْكَارُ الصَّبَاح
Adhkar Al-Sabah

Morning Adhkar

اَللّٰهُمَّ بِكَ أَصْبَحْنَا وَبِكَ أَمْسَيْنَا

"O Allah, by You we reach the morning and by You we reach the evening, by You we live and by You we die, and to You is the resurrection."

Morning Opening Supplication
✦ Best Time
Recite Between Fajr and Sunrise

The morning adhkār should ideally be recited between Fajr and sunrise. If unable to recite them in this time, you may still recite them afterwards. If unable to complete all, be consistent with at least some.

⏱ After Fajr → Until Sunrise

"Whoever says in the morning and evening: 'Glory be to Allah and with His praise' one hundred times, no one will come on the Day of Resurrection with anything better than what he came with, except someone who said the same or more."

Sahih Muslim
