أَذْكَارُ الصَّبَاح
Adhkar Al-Sabah

Morning Adhkar

اَللّٰهُمَّ بِكَ أَصْبَحْنَا وَبِكَ أَمْسَيْنَا

"O Allah, by You we reach the morning and by You we reach the evening, by You we live and by You we die, and to You is the resurrection."

Morning Opening Supplication
✦ Best Time
Recite Between Fajr and Sunrise

The morning adhkār should ideally be recited between Fajr and sunrise. If unable to recite them in this time, you may still recite them afterwards.

⏱ After Fajr → Until Sunrise
