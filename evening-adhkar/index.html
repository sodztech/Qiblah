أَذْكَارُ الْمَسَاء
Adhkar Al-Masa'

Evening Adhkar

اَللّٰهُمَّ بِكَ أَمْسَيْنَا وَبِكَ أَصْبَحْنَا

"O Allah, by You we reach the evening and by You we reach the morning, by You we live and by You we die, and to You is the resurrection."

Evening Opening Supplication
✦ Best Time
Recite Between ʿAsr and Maghrib

The evening adhkār should ideally be recited between ʿAsr and Maghrib. If unable to recite them in this time, you may still recite them afterwards. If unable to complete all, be consistent with at least some.

⏱ After ʿAsr → Until Maghrib Adhaan

"Whoever says in the morning and evening: 'Glory be to Allah and with His praise' one hundred times, no one will come on the Day of Resurrection with anything better than what he came with, except someone who said the same or more."

Sahih Muslim
